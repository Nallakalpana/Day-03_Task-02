TAJ MAHAL

The Taj Mahal is a magnificent white marble mausoleum located in Agra, India. It was built by the Mughal Emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his beloved wife Mumtaz Mahal and completed in 1653. Known as a symbol of eternal love, the monument is famous for its stunning architecture, intricate carvings, and beautiful gardens. It is one of the Seven Wonders of the World and a UNESCO World Heritage Site, attracting millions of visitors from around the globe every year.
